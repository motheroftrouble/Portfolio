Hi, I'm Paula. Here's a bit about me.


I am currently enrolled in an accelerated online Bachelor of Science program at East Coast Polytechnic Institute (ECPI) University, pursuing a degree in Electrical Systems Engineering Technology in Mechatronics. This program was a natural choice, blending the electrical theory and mechanical engineering principles that have long been integral to my aviation career—including more than 25 years of avionics and electrical troubleshooting and repair, which I began in November 1998—with the programming and technical problem-solving skills I’ve grown passionate about. I bring strong leadership abilities to every team I join, motivating and developing others while leading by example and inspiring high performance.


As I advance in my engineering journey, I am driven by a passion for designing innovative, practical solutions that improve quality of life while minimizing environmental impact. I thrive on my out-of-the-box thinking and strong brainstorming abilities, approaching every challenge with creativity and purpose. I enjoy creating systems and products that prioritize sustainability, comfort, efficiency, and long-term positive impact—striving to align engineering principles as closely as possible with permaculture ideals.


I am excited to contribute my abilities within a progressive engineering or technology-focused organization where curiosity, innovation, and continuous improvement are valued. I bring a strong commitment to learning, adaptability in dynamic environments, and the ability to translate complex ideas into practical, meaningful outcomes. With a balanced blend of technical training, real-world experience, and a genuine desire to create solutions that matter, I am prepared to support ambitious projects and grow into increasingly challenging roles.
